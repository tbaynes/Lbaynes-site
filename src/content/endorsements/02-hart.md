A marvelous study: scrupulous, illuminating, and much needed. In part, it goes some way toward rescuing Lewis from many of those who admire him but who have a curiously confused notion of how he viewed Scripture. It also provides a portrait of the development of his thinking that, as far as I know, no one else has given us.
